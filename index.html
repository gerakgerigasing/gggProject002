Who We Are

Bridging Vision and Reality

Gerak Geri Gasing is a multidisciplinary creative studio dedicated to the art of storytelling and artistic excellence.

Founded on the belief that art should move people—literally and emotionally—we operate at the intersection of production, consultation, and management. Our name, inspired by the traditional spinning top, represents constant motion, balance, and the artistry found in purposeful movement.

Based in Southeast Asia with a global perspective, we collaborate with artists, brands, and institutions to create work that resonates across cultures and mediums.
